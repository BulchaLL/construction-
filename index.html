About Us

MDC Construction is a growing construction company based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, committed to delivering high-quality and reliable construction solutions. With years of experience in the industry, we specialize in residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects, offering end-to-end services from planning and design to execution and completion.Our team is dedicated to precision, safety, and professionalism—ensuring every project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standard. We take pride in building durable structures and long-lasting relationships with our clients through transparency, quality workmanship, and exceptional service.
